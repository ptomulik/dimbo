DIMBO project supports our development of novel methods for modeling and analysis of general constrained multibody systems. The scope of the project includes strategies for co-simulation (restricted to multibody systems) and implementation of efficient algorithms on GPU-based platform (OpenCL backend). To allow experimenting with certain parts of DIMBO code we provide bindings to scripting languages (currently Python is supported).
